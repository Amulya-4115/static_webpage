Types of Laptops

Explore the various kinds of laptops available in the market

Gaming Laptop

High-performance laptops with powerful GPUs and cooling systems, ideal for gaming and heavy tasks.

Business Laptop

Lightweight and secure laptops designed for professionals, with long battery life and durability.

Student Laptop

Affordable laptops with essential features for study, notes, browsing, and basic tasks.

Macbook

Thin, lightweight laptops with high-speed SSDs and excellent portability, ideal for travel.

2-in-1 Laptop

Hybrid laptops with touchscreen displays that can be used as tablets, offering flexibility.
